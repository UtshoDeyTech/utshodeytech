## What I'm Working On

Currently a Senior Software Engineer at LEADS Corporation Limited, where I build and ship compliance technology for Bangladesh's banking sector.

My core focus is BankUltimus CBS — a Core Banking System with an embedded AML and CFT detection engine that helps financial institutions monitor transactions, detect suspicious patterns, and stay ahead of regulatory requirements set by the Bangladesh Financial Intelligence Unit.

On any given day I'm working across backend rule engines, Angular-based compliance dashboards, graph-based relationship detection for trade finance fraud, and presenting the product to executives and compliance officers at major Bangladeshi financial institutions.

Beyond banking compliance, I have built across a range of domains — from AI-powered document intelligence platforms and social media automation tools, to insurance technology pipelines and machine learning driven fraud detection systems. ### Tailored House Price Prediction Insights for Dhaka and Chittagong City

**Published in:** Springer Lecture Notes in Electrical Engineering, ICEEE 2024 · January 2025

**Authors:** Utsho Dey, Md. Sakhawat Hossain Rabbi, Md. Abrar Hamim, Md. Tarek Habib

This research applies machine learning techniques — including XGBoost, Random Forest, and Linear Regression — to predict real estate prices across Dhaka and Chittagong. The study leverages a comprehensive dataset sourced from bdproperty.com, analyzing key features such as location, property specifications, and temporal patterns to deliver city-level prediction insights relevant to Bangladesh's property market.

[View on Springer](https://link.springer.com/chapter/10.1007/978-981-97-9112-5_14) · [View on ResearchGate](https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Utsho-Dey)
